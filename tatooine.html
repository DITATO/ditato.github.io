Tatooine is a desert planet that is belived to be one of the oldest planets in the Star Wars Universe. Jungles and large oceans coverd the planet the planet was inhabited by the Kumugah a very advanced race. The Kumugah colonized other nearby planets. The Rakata found out and decided to take over Tatooine. When the Rakatas empire began to fall the Kumugah decided to fight back. This angerd the Rakata and they decided to bomb the planet. After the bombs hit the planet the soil became sand and the oceans disapered. The Kumugah split into two differnt races the Tusken Raiders and Jawas. Now many people on Tatooine are farmers and traders. Tatooine is next to a major hyperspacce route and has many crowded cities.
